Summary: Latitude vs. X

Latitude is the Web's foremost authority for in-depth examination of the relationship between latitude and climate. The purpose of Latitude is to analyze how weather changes as we move closer to the equator.

Data for more than 500 cities was compiled through the OpenWeatherAPI. Using the dataset, we utilized matplotlib, to plot various relationships of weather to latitude including:

  • Cloudiness
  • Humidity
  • Max Temperature
  • Wind Speed

Latitude includes the source data that was compiled in this analysis, as well as the output visualizations. We also offer explanations and descriptions of trends we discovered in the course of our work.
